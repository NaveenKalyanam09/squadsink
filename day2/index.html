National Film Awards

Here are the Best Actor awardees from the Indian National Film Awards for each year starting from 2019:

67th National Film Awards(for films of 2019)

  • Announced: 22 March 2021
  • Presented: 25 October 2021
  • Best Feature Film: Marakkar: Lion of the Arabian Sea
  • Best Actor: Dhanush
  • Best Actress: Kangana Ranaut

68th National Film Awards(for films of 2020)

  • Announced: 22 July 2022
  • Presented: Held subsequently, awardees recognized
  • Best Feature Film: Soorarai Pottru
  • Best Actor: Suriya
  • Best Actress: Aparna Balamural

69th National Film Awards(for films of 2021)

  • Announced: 24 August 2023
  • Presented: 17 October 2023
  • Best Feature Film: Rocketry: The Nambi Effect
  • Best Actor: Allu Arjun First Telugu actor to win Best Actor at National Awards
  • Best Actress: Alia Bhatt
